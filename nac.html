The New Administrative Capitol Project

The largest infrastructure project in all of Tabantha's history; deep in the heart of Poconos.

Phase I

InterC highways (White Highways) will be built in the New Tabantha Zone, and Senate and House buildings will be built and finished. City Limits will be established, and official governmental functions will be moved to the New Administrative Capitol.

Phase II

Older highways will be renovated and adjusted for newer infrastructure, and connections to InterC highways will be built. A new InterC highway will be built from the City of Poconos to Tabantha City with an interchange in New Tabantha; and government offices will be relocated from Tabantha and built in the New Administrative Capitol.

Phase III

An InterC beltway will be built around the perimeter of the city, and grants will be given for residential properties to individuals and conglomerates; and the official capitol will be moved ceremonially to the New Administrative Capitol and the official name, City of New Tabantha; will be set.
